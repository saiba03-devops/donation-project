Ensuring Education for Every Child: India’s Trusted Education NGO

At Donation, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education and equal opportunities to succeed. Our mission is to support underprivileged children by providing educational resources, care, and guidance that help build a brighter future.

Our Mission: Supporting Children Through Donations & Care

As a trusted child donation and welfare organization, we are dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children through education, healthcare, nutrition, and emotional support.

Here are the key areas where your donations make an impact:

Education Support

Donations help provide books, uniforms, and education support for children in need.

Food & Nutrition

We ensure children receive healthy meals and proper nutrition for a better future.

Healthcare Support

Donations support medical checkups, medicines, and healthcare services.

Child Protection

We provide shelter, safety, and care for homeless and orphaned children.
